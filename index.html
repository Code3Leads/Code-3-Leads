About Code 3 Leads

Code 3 Leads was built to help contractors stop losing business from missed calls. When you're on a job site, driving, or tied up with another customer, every missed call can mean a missed opportunity.

As a veteran-owned business, Code 3 Leads was created with a focus on reliability, responsiveness, and helping service-based businesses capture more leads without adding more work to their day.

Our goal is simple: help contractors respond faster, win more jobs, and stop losing revenue from missed calls.

If you’re busy on a job, driving, or after hours, the call is missed.

3. Automatic Text Response
