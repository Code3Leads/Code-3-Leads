Code 3 Leads Privacy Policy

Last Updated: March 10, 2026

Code 3 Leads respects your privacy and is committed to protecting the information you share with us.

Information We Collect

When customers contact contractors using the Code 3 Leads system, we may collect information such as:

  • Name
  • Phone number
  • Service address
  • Description of the service request

This information is used solely to deliver service requests to the contractor and facilitate communication between the customer and the contractor.

How Information Is Used

The information collected through the Code 3 Leads system is used to:

  • Deliver service requests to contractors
  • Notify contractors of customer inquiries
  • Improve the performance of the service

We do not sell or share personal information with third parties for marketing purposes.

Messaging and SMS Communication

Customers may receive automated text messages when contacting a contractor using Code 3 Leads. Message frequency may vary depending on the interaction. Standard message and data rates may apply.

Data Security

We take reasonable steps to protect the information submitted through our system.
