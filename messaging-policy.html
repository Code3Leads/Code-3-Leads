Code 3 Leads Messaging Policy

Effective Date: March 10, 2026

Code 3 Leads uses SMS messaging to help contractors respond to missed calls and customer inquiries more efficiently.

How Messaging Works

When a customer contacts a contractor using the Code 3 Leads system, the customer may receive an automated text message asking for additional information about their service request.

Message Frequency

Message frequency may vary depending on the customer’s interaction and the contractor’s workflow.

Message and Data Rates

Standard message and data rates may apply depending on the customer’s mobile carrier and plan.

Consent

By providing a phone number and engaging with a contractor using the Code 3 Leads system, users consent to receive relevant automated text messages related to their inquiry.

Opt-Out

Users may opt out of receiving messages at any time by replying STOP. After opting out, no further messages will be sent unless the user initiates contact again.

Help

For help, users may reply HELP or contact us directly at Code3Leads@gmail.com.

Privacy

Information collected through messaging is handled in accordance with our Privacy Policy.
